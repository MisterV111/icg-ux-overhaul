Program Overview

The Yousician For Schools pilot represented a groundbreaking approach to music education, leveraging technology to make learning instruments accessible and engaging for young students across Finland's capital region.

Over six months, we coordinated the delivery of instruments, Yousician software licenses, and supporting hardware to ten elementary schools. We developed comprehensive educator training workshops, created instructional video resources, and built a scalable framework that could be replicated across additional schools and regions.

The program demonstrated that technology-enhanced music education could effectively complement traditional teaching methods, providing students with interactive, self-paced learning tools while maintaining the guidance and mentorship of classroom educators.
